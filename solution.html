The Solution

What is the solution? What will the final product look like?

Style Tile

After combining all of the material gathered so far from research, the creative phase of the project may now begin, with the style tile being the first to be created. To begin the style tile, we first brainstorm adjectives that represent the website and the organization. The following are some adjectives for UrbanGlass: Energetic, Inviting, Modern, Clean, Delicate, and Informative

Based on the adjectives listed above, more attributes are considered regarding the patterns/textures, colors, typefaces, buttons, and layout, all of which are then assembled into the Style Tile. I wanted the images on the redesigned website to have the effect of transparency when scrolling down the page which would symbolize the transparent and delicate nature of glass. I kept the original color of red in the redign with the added color of blue for a striking contrast between the two but still express invitation to viewers.

Call to Action

The Call to Action button would be the buttons that prompts the user to perform a specific action which is to click on them which transfers the user to specific pages on the website. It should be graphically pleasing soÂ the fewer the words, the stronger. The position should be simple and logical, with a large enough contrast to capture attention while the text should be straightforward and easy to grasp.

The UrbanGlass organization has two main audiences which are those wishing to donate to their cause while the other is those wishing to know more about the organization and potentially join a course or classes so based on these two main audiences, I created two buttons while the donation being more important while the mission is second. To make the donation button more important, I made the button a main color with white text while the mission button is clear in the background with colored text.
